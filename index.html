Why luxury fashion brands?

The Elite, The Value, The Quality

When it comes to fashion brands, we all know fast fashion brands like Zara, H&M, Shein, ASOS, etc. On the other hands, there is also the luxury fashion. Luxury fashion brands have been talked about all the world, and the reason why luxury fashion has been in the markey for centuries is because of the quality, the exclusivity and thr status.

But why should you care about luxury fashion? It is the quality and the craftsmanship that comes with each item. Perhaps you will use that fast fashion t-shirt 10+ times until it is worn out, that is okay. However, a sweater that costs $30 might work as well as a sweater from Hermes that costs nearly 10 times more, but it is the quality and the craftsmanship that cannot be compared. Final thought, luxury fashion is not for everyone, but once you go that route, it is difficult to get out.
